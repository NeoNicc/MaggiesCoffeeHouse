Welcome to Luverne's Coziest Cafe

Located in downtown Luverne, Alabama, Maggie's Coffee House offers fresh espresso, breakfast, lunch, pastries, smoothies and more! We are committed to providing a cozy atmosphere and delicious food and drinks for our community.

Specials

Espresso Drinks

From classic lattes to bold americanos, our espresso drinks are crafted with care and quality beans.

Breakfast & Lunch

Enjoy our hearty breakfast sandwiches, fresh salads, and delicious lunch options made with local ingredients.

Pastries & More

Indulge in our selection of pastries, muffins, and sweet treats, perfect for pairing with your coffee.

Our Menu

Explore our diverse menu featuring everything from classic espresso drinks to hearty breakfast sandwiches and fresh salads. We use high-quality ingredients to ensure every bite and sip is a delight.
